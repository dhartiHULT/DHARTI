PHASE 1


In first phase we'll work towards Establishing a base, interconnecting communities and providing them accurate and useful information.
Stream of income in this stage will be primarily be through personalized Ads and funding provided.



Following are the steps company will take:

  • Chat (multilevel), group chats whose admins can then represent those chats in principal chats. Connections like Friends on Facebook, and chat option available in multiple languages and speech too. Relevant posts available on custom wall and real time translate available with speech support.

  • Speech access and local language support will be provided.

  • Data regarding geographical location, soil quality and crop planted will be collected to customize the user experience and showcase better info to the farmer.

  • Daily relevant news and short summary of relevant Schemes will be provided.

  • Accurate short-term weather info and approximate long-term climate projections will also be displayed.

  • Daily feed from local and national relevant news, info, advertisements and connections will be generated in local language, both text and speech based.

  • Tips based upon current crops will be generated and displayed on the daily feed too.

  • Platform to collaborate, share resources and maintain shared bank accounts to pool in resources.

  • Connections to local and national farmer help lines will be provided.

  • List of authorized seed and raw material distributors will be included and information regarding false and banned seeds will be broadcasted to make farmers aware about malpractices and their penalties.

  • Crop list recommendation based upon geographical data, market demands, seasons and budget will be provided.

  • One tip a day feature.

  • Help farmers to connect with NGOs and govt agencies to avail their rightful benefits.
