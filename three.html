PHASE 3


In third phase we'll work towards establishment of R&D departments, training centers, logistics department and shop outlets.
Company will start acquiring landmass to setup its own advanced modern farms with skilled rural youth.
National agriculture researches will be encouraged and supported.
Fests and Events will also be organized that’ll bring in some additional revenue.
Revenue at this stage will be derived from direct sales and services, along with previous streams.



Following are the steps company will take:

  • Recommendation of crops based upon intelligent feedback systems, through the extensive geographical and market data collected.

  • Partnership with telecommunication companies to increase the penetration of internet and technology in rural India.

  • R & D units will be established where better farming techniques, seeds etc. will be researched.

  • Manufacturing plants will be setup that’ll provide raw materials at competitively lowered prices to increase affordability and enhance market penetration.

  • The Company will transition into a wholesale buyer, buying goods directly from farmers at sustainable prices and company shall also start maintaining stock buffer at this point.

  • The company will establish institutes where agricultural and rural youth will be trained and made aware about modern farming techniques such as vertical faming etc. and the Company will also acquire landmass to employ such modern green farming techniques.

  • Urban vendors will also be absorbed into the system as shopkeepers for outlets the Company intends to setup. They will enjoy several employee and elevated benefits compared to their previous positions. Benefits include but are not limited to – (Job security and steady income, better working conditions and higher wages)

  • Company at this stage will also fund relevant researches in agricultural field.

  • The company also intends to join hands with various NGOs and Govt. organizations at this stage to provide support to Indian farmers.

  • The company shall also introduce its nationwide logistics department at this stage.

  • Local and National fests will be organized where the farmer will be exposed to new farming ideas and will be able to present himself/herself too. These fests will also help establish brand image and will generate income through advertisers and presenters’ fees.
