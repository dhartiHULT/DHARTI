PHASE 2


In second phase we'll be Providing free and paid educational content, relevant to modern farming needs, establishing support centers, setting up of online market place and generating sales.
Revenue in this phase will be generated through content subscription, commissioned sales (along with previous streams) and relevant data will be collected.

  • At this phase signed up sellers and logistic companies will be able to display ads and contact farming communities and individual farmers.
